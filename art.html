Art Practice
At university, my art coursework was concentrated in digital design. This has translated well to graphic design in the workplace, but keeping up my personal art practice allows me to get more creative and try out new techniques. Because architecture is an obession of mine and I'm always looking up at buildings, I began making architectural art pieces a few years ago. My style has become distinct, taking architectural details from around a city to digitally illustrate and piece together.

Makers to Modern

Makers to Modern explores the connection between modernity and tradition and the rich history of the world. Modernity is addressed through illustrated compositions based on the architecture of some of the most modern cities in the world: Surveying Dubai, Sydney, Tokyo, Santiago, San Francisco, and Copenhagen. Tradition is addressed through the frames, backed and wrapped in fabric (four of which I designed) based on traditional artisanries from the respective region each piece belongs to.
