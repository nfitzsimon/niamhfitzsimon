Graphic Design
I focused my art major at university on digital design, which included 2D design, typography, 2D animation, and vector classes. My college job involved graphic design work and I was the sole graphic designer (as well as being other things) at MyVR. While my artwork has a very specific voice, I try to incorporate variation in my graphic design work and push myself to discover new techniques and styles.
