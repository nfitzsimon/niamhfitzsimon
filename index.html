I spent most of my college career focused on becoming a software engineer, taking the appropriate courses and spending my summers as a software engineer intern at Google. Adding an art major to my coursework was only a way to develop the creative thinking that is integral to being a great engineer. However, during my final art course I began exploring multidisciplinary paths. Around that time, I was accepted into the Priya Haji Fellowship with True Ventures and paired with MyVR. At MyVR I was given the chance to work as a software engineer and graphic designer. There was a natural transition into taking on User Interface and User Experience Design.
